Target Audience

Target Audience:

This site is geared towards community members and leaders. Those that wish to place events on a calendar have the ability to do so. Those that live in the community visiting the site will be looking for weather forecast and upcoming activities.

Personas:

Persona #1: Brad is a football coach of a peewee football team that practices in a local field regularly.

Persona #2: Jessica has just moved into the neighborhood.

Scenarios:

Scenario #1: Brad is out shopping and forgot to check the weather for his teams practice this afternoon.

Scenario #2: Jessica would like to get to know some of her neighbors. She checks out the Weather & Beyond site to see what upcoming events she could attend.

Scenario #3: Brad would like to set up practice times for his team but would like to ensure that nothing else is going on that could be a scheudle conflict for his team.

Scenario #4: Brad would like to make sure all the parents know when and where practice is. He doesn't have all their phone numbers so he uses the calendar to schedule practice times where the parents can see them.

Scenario #5: Jessica has made a couple of friends but would like to put together a block party to get to know some more people. She puts it on the calendar to make sure all her neighbors know. She also is able to find a date where the weather looks promising.
