Tours and Locations

Here are some noteworthy locations to visit during your stay in Cozumel.
We also offer guided tours to these locations for groups and individuals. Ask us about it in your reservation or during your visit.

Zona Arqueológica San Gervasio

Visit a real archaeological site of a Maya civilization. The site is located in the center of the north end of the island. You will be able to view plazas, buildings and even some hand prints painted on the walls of the Las Minitas ruin.

Playa Palancar

One of our most beautiful beaches, Playa Palancar is located on the south western most tip of the island. Lay back and enjoy the beautiful views, get a natural tan. If you are in the mood for more adventure, you can rent a kayak or snorkling gear right there on the beach.

Museum of Cozumel

Want to know more about our beautiful island's history and culture, visit our Museum of Cozumel. Located in Centro Cozumel down the street of our Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry location.

Coral Reefs Monument

If you are renting from our Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry location, then your first stop must be the Coral Reefs Monument just down the street. Capture stunning photos with the beautiful ocean and sky as a backdrop.

Punta Sur Beach

A bit of a drive, but well worth it is the Punta Sur Eco Beach Park. Near the southernmost tip of the island, this beach is a natural paradise. And if you are in the mood, you can take a short tour to observe crocodiles in their natural habitat.

Faro Celarain Lighthouse

Truly at the south tip of the island is the Faro Celarain lighthouse. Climb the lighthouse for scenic views and once in a lifetime photos. You will be able to see views of the beach, ocean and the ecological reserve.
