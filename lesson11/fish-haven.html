A Beautiful Day in Preston

We had a great day here in Preston last Friday. The weather was a comfortable 68 degrees fahrenheit. There was a light breeze. At many of the local parks you could see many people flying kites. It was an unusual site this late in the year. Just evidence that Preston is the best city to live in.
